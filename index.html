FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What size organizations does Neptora typically work with?
+

We work with organizations of all sizes — from well-funded startups building their first product to Fortune 500 enterprises running global transformation programs. Our engagement models are tailored to your scale and budget.

How long does a typical engagement last?
+

Engagements range from 6-week strategic sprints to multi-year transformation partnerships. Most of our new clients start with a focused discovery engagement (4–6 weeks) before committing to a longer-term program. We strongly believe in earning the right to expand.

Do you work on a fixed-fee or time-and-materials basis?
+

We offer both models. For well-scoped projects, we prefer fixed-fee arrangements that align incentives and give clients certainty. For evolving engagements, we use transparent time-and-materials with weekly reporting. We're always flexible and will recommend the model that makes most sense for your situation.

Can Neptora work alongside our existing in-house team?
+

Absolutely — in fact, this is how we prefer to work. Embedded collaboration with your team ensures knowledge transfer, faster delivery, and more sustainable outcomes. We bring specialist expertise; you bring institutional knowledge. Together we move faster.

What industries do you specialize in?
+

We have deep experience in financial services, healthcare, technology, retail, energy, and the public sector. Our approach is fundamentally about the nature of the problem, not the industry — which means our thinking often translates valuable cross-sector insights to each new domain.
